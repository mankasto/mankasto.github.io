    <h2>About Me</h2>


    <p>

      I am currently a Perception Algorithm Engineer at <a href="https://www.weride.ai">Weride</a>, a Tier-1 L4 autonomous driving company, where I work on traffic light detection and state estimation systems. I received my M.S. degree in Cyberspace Security from <a href="https://cst.buaa.edu.cn">Beihang University (BUAA)</a> in 2024, and my B.S. degree from the same university in 2021. During my master's study, I was a research intern at Tsinghua University working on wafer defect detection. I also spent time at Weride as an intern before joining full-time.

    </p>


    <p>

      My current research interest focuses on <strong>World Models</strong>, particularly their applications in autonomous driving and embodied AI. I am interested in how world models can enable better understanding, prediction, and decision-making in complex dynamic environments.

    </p>
